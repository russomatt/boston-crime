This project is a visualization of Boston crime data from August 2015 to January 2018, which was originally plotted in QGIS using datasets gathered from the City of Boston and Massachusetts GIS website. In order to allow for a granular view of the frequency of crime incidents across the city, they are visualized as a heat map showing the actual distribution of crime hotspots across neighborhood boundaries. For a more in-depth analysis of crime in Boston, factors that have previously been identified to affect crime have been plotted onto the map. You can interact with the legend to learn more about the individual factors and toggle the display of the heat map.
